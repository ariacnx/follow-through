# Message Templates

These are starting templates. Always check `voice-notes.md` first and adapt to user's actual voice.

## LinkedIn DMs by Action Tier

### ☕ Coffee Invite
> Hey [Name]! [Great meeting/connecting] at [Event]. [1 personal touch about them or conversation]. Let's [stay in touch/definitely connect]! If you're ever around Sunnyvale or Mountain View, let me know — I'd love to grab a coffee ☕

### 🤝 Collab (requires user-provided context)
> Hey [Name]! [Great meeting/connecting] at [Event] — [reference specific conversation topic user provided]. [Connect to own related project/interest]. [Specific collab ask or next step]. Let's definitely stay in touch! [Optional: coffee invite if local]

### 👋 Stay in Touch (English)
> Hey [Name]! [Great meeting/connecting] at [Event]. Let's stay in touch, I'll share any fun events I come across! 😊

### 👋 Stay in Touch (Chinese)
> hii [name] 很喜欢你们那天做的产品！我们以后多多交流啊，有好的活动我也和你分享！
